Nonograms are played on a grid of rows and columns, aiming to unveil a hidden pattern.

Let’s get started!

Numerical clues at the start of rows and columns guide the placement of filled squares.

Each clue number represents consecutive filled squares with at least one empty square between groups.

Click left mouse button on the square to fill.

Click right mouse button on the square to be empty.

Use logic and deduction to progressively fill the grid row by row and column by column and ensure correct placement of filled and empty squares to complete these puzzles.
